Privacy Policy for EasyCDR

Last updated: February 12, 2026

EasyCDR is a 100% offline Call Detail Record (CDR) analysis tool designed for law enforcement and investigative use.

1. Data Collection and Usage

- EasyCDR does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, device information, or user activity.

- All CDR files and databases are processed locally on your device only.

- No data is sent to any server, third party, or developer.

- No analytics, ads, or tracking libraries are used.

2. Permissions

- The app only requests storage permission to read CDR files.

- No other permissions (location, contacts, camera, etc.) are used.

3. Activation & Unlock

- Full version unlock (unlimited rows in Primary CDR) is a one-time payment of ₹500.

- Activation is manual via WhatsApp contact with the developer.

- Device ID is displayed locally for activation — it is not sent automatically.

4. Security

Your CDR data never leaves your device.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here.
